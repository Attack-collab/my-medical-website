About Me

I'm Dr. Ataklti, a medical doctor with experience in treating a wide range of diseases and providing humanitarian services in conflict-affected areas of Tigray, Ethiopia. My work has spanned district hospitals and humanitarian settings, where I've treated patients of all ages and backgrounds, including those impacted by tropical diseases, non-communicable diseases, and conflict-related health issues.

Medical Services

I provide comprehensive medical care for all age groups, specializing in tropical diseases, non-communicable diseases, and emergency care. My experience includes working in both hospital settings and remote, conflict-affected areas.

Humanitarian Work

During my time with MSF Belgium, I worked to provide free healthcare services to conflict-affected internally displaced persons (IDPs) and communities in remote areas. My focus has been on emergency care, neonatology, and pediatrics.
